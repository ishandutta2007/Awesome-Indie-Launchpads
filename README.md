# Awesome-Indie-Launchpads
## Top Indie Project Launchpads Ecosystem

**Curated List of SaaS Products & Open-Source GitHub Projects**  
*Focused on Indie Maker Launch Platforms, Directories & Product Hunt Alternatives*  
**Last updated: March 2026**

This repository tracks notable **SaaS platforms** and **open-source projects** for **Indie Project Launchpads**. These tools help solo founders, indie hackers, and small teams launch, showcase, and gain traction for their side projects, SaaS tools, apps, and open-source creations through directories, communities, upvotes, and feedback.

**Examples** include Sidehunt, Uneed, Indie Hackers, Hacker News, Dev Hunt, BetaList, and AppSumo (the category leaders). Tools listed here emphasize **visibility**, community feedback, launch-day amplification, and maker-friendly workflows.

**Open-source emphasis**: This section is heavily expanded with every major active project for self-hosting, community-driven directories, custom launch platforms, and full control — ideal for indie communities, DAOs, and developers who want open and sovereign launch ecosystems.

Contributions welcome! Open a PR to add/update entries. Keep descriptions factual and link to official sites.

### Core Platforms (Indie Launchpads)

- **[Sidehunt](https://sidehunt.com/)**  
  Dedicated platform for launching and discovering indie side projects with strong community voting and feedback.

- **[Uneed](https://uneed.best/)**  
  Modern indie product launch platform focused on tools, apps, and SaaS with curated exposure.

- **[Indie Hackers](https://www.indiehackers.com/)**  
  The original community and launch platform for bootstrapped founders sharing journeys and products.

- **[Hacker News](https://news.ycombinator.com/)**  
  Iconic tech community where many indie projects gain massive visibility through "Show HN" posts.

- **[Dev Hunt](https://devhunt.org/)**  
  Developer-focused launch platform for tools, libraries, and indie developer products.

- **[BetaList](https://betalist.com/)**  
  Long-standing platform for launching and discovering early-stage startups and indie projects.

- **[AppSumo](https://appsumo.com/)**  
  Marketplace for launching and selling lifetime deals to a large audience of indie makers and entrepreneurs.
